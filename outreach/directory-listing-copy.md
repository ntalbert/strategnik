## 3. Short Bio (150 words)

Strategnik is a fractional CMO and go-to-market architecture practice for Series A through C B2B SaaS companies. Founded by Nick Talbert, the practice applies the Physics of Growth framework to diagnose pipeline problems at their root and build the systems that solve them. Unlike traditional advisory engagements, Strategnik operates inside the business -- owning strategy, building the infrastructure, standing up teams, and running execution until the function is self-sustaining. Core engagements include GTM strategy and architecture, ICP development and validation, demand generation build-out, AEO/SEO content architecture, RevOps alignment, and positioning and messaging. Clients are typically post-product-market-fit companies where the GTM motion has stalled, the pipeline is unpredictable, or the marketing function needs senior leadership without a full-time hire. Strategnik brings the strategic depth of a CMO with the operational intensity of a builder.

## 6. Nick Talbert -- Personal Bio (150 words)

Nick Talbert is a fractional CMO and GTM architect with more than twenty years of experience building and scaling go-to-market functions for B2B technology companies. His career spans adtech (Adapt.tv/AOL, Sizmek/Amazon), martech (Quartile), and B2B SaaS (Certent, BRIA AI), where he has held VP Marketing and CMO roles responsible for pipeline, positioning, and revenue growth. He developed the Physics of Growth framework to bring structure and repeatability to the GTM challenges growth-stage companies face between product-market fit and scale. Through Strategnik, he works as an embedded operator inside Series A through C B2B SaaS companies -- building strategy, standing up teams, and running execution rather than delivering slide decks. He focuses on the problems that actually stall growth: unclear ICP, broken pipeline mechanics, misaligned RevOps, and weak positioning.
